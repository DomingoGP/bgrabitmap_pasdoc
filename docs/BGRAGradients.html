Types

TRectangleMapOption = (...);

Phong shading functions. Use a height map (grayscale image or a precise map filled with MapHeightToBGRA) to determine orientation and position of the surface.

Phong shading consist in adding an ambiant light, a diffuse light (angle between light and object), and a specular light (angle between light, object and observer, i.e. reflected light)
